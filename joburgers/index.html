Hi, we're JO's. Passionate about farming and a love for strawberries.

Founded in the heart of lush farmland, Jo's the business began its journey as a humble family-owned strawberry patch in the early 2000s, with Jo at the helm.

What started as a small endeavor fueled by Jo's passion for farming and a love for strawberries quickly blossomed into a thriving business.

Life is sweet with berrylicious treats!
